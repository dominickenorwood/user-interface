Le Kinkeliba enables local African doctors to combat maternal and infant mortality and perform a range of essential services. Now we need your generous support to continue this vital work. Your contributions will pay for life-saving medical supplies and for the creation of additional facilities that will palpably save lives. Thanks to financial support that has already been provided to cover our own administrative expenses, we can assure you that whatever you give will go directly to the benefit people in need in Africa.
