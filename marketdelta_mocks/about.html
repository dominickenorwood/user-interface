MarketDelta® provides charting and analytics software to professional traders around the world.

MarketDelta® provides charting and analytics software to professional traders around the world. Our Footprint® chart put us on the map. The level of price transparency it provides is unrivaled and recognized as an innovative leap forward for active traders seeking better price transparency, better visuals for the markets they trade, and algorithmic trading idea development.

We have 3 primary business segments.

  1. Our core product, MarketDelta® desktop software, was developed with discretionary traders in mind and is our core business.
  2. Licensing the Footprint® to other vendors.
  3. Taking our ideas and concepts and partnering with top tier algorithmic trading software providers to market the concepts to those trading algorithmically.

Commitment

MarketDelta® is committed to not just innovating and providing the best software, but we are passionate about helping our subscribers learn the software and educate them on how to apply it effectively in a myriad of ways.

Company Information

MarketDelta LLC is a technology and software as a service company based in the Chicagoland area. Our success stems from sheer determination to offer out something different to traders and exploit developments brought about from electronic markets. MarketDelta® fits the needs of professional traders worldwide by providing a way to "See Inside the Chart®" and yield better price transparency.

Intellectual Property

MarketDelta® protects its intellectual property through patent, copyright, and trademark. In an effort to make our technology available to other vendors, companies, developers, and individuals, we created a license agreement that allows the Footprint® chart to be licensed, integrated, and re-sold. Please visit this page for more information.
