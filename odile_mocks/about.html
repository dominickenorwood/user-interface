About Odile

A luxury children's boutique

We provides a welcoming sanctuary in the heart of Chicago's Gold Coast, stocked with the best European children's wear available. Find exclusive clothing, shoes and keepsake gifts for every occasion.

visit the storefront

if you are near our store, please stop in to see the Odile collection. 710 North Wabash.

It started with a trip to Florence!

Camille Cozzini always dreamt of opening a children's clothing boutique where she could sell the kind of clothing she wore as a child. "I saw a need in the US market for quality, traditional children's wear that would transcend trends and focus on dressing children like children."

Travelling twice a year to Italy, Camille diligently scouts out the best children's wear that Europe has to offer. Most of her finds are exclusive to either the United States or Chicago area. Odile provides a welcoming sanctuary in the heart of Chicago's Gold Coast. Stocked with the best European children's wear available, the boutique offers its loyal customers a personalized shopping experience and exceptional customer service.

Camille Cozzini, Shop Owner
