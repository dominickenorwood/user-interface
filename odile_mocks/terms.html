Privacy Policy

Your Information

Be assured Odile does not sell or share customer information associated with orders or email sign up with any third parties.

Ordering online

All purchases are made using our secure online server. For security purposes we do not store your credit card information. Our checkout process is as simple as can be so that you can purchase multiple items filling out the least amount of information necessary.

Email Sign up

In an effort to be as eco friendly as possible, Odile offers an email sign up. By adding your name to the list, you are agreeing that Odile can use your email to send you information on Odile product updates, Sales and other Odile events. Please note, we do not share your email with third parties.
