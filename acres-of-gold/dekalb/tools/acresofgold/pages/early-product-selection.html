Product Selection

Evaluating Early Season Stand Establishment

Understanding differences in emergence and early season growth between corn products on your farm can offer valuable insights into potential growth and yield differences later in the season.

Genetic Differences

  • Are you seeing differences in emergence and early vigor? Could they be related to differences in product ratings for emergence and seedling growth?

Environment

  • Was the seedbed ideal? ... cold and wet? ... too dry?

Residue

  • Did excessive residue keep the soil cool and wet?
  • Did residue interfere with planting operations resulting in uneven seeding depth?
  • Is residue decomposition tying up nutrients (most notably Nitrogen) needed by the crop?

Disease

  • Are there observable differences in the presence of seedling diseases between products?
  • What about early season foliar diseases such as Anthracnose Leaf Blight?

Answering these questions can help not only identify issues this season, but can aid in making better product selections for corn-on-corn fields on your farm next year.
