Step 6

Harvest

Harvest represents both an end and a new beginning. As the combines roll and this year's growing season draws to a close, we get to both celebrate and evaluate our successes. But key opportunities to begin setting the stage for next year's crop abound. Whether it's capturing accurate yield data from this year's crop to inform product decisions, collecting soil samples to determine nutrient needs or managing residue, next year's success starts now.
