Disease Management

The Importance of Scouting

Scouting near the VT growth stage is important to determine if a fungicide application is needed. Scouting is essential each year, not only to determine management needs for the current year's crop, but also to identify potential disease risks for the following year.

Fungicide Applications

Product response in the presence of disease can be associated with:

  • Seed disease tolerance/susceptibility.
  • Plant placement (corn-on-corn, fields with history of disease pressure, irrigated environments, etc.).
  • Timing of application relative to crop and disease development.
