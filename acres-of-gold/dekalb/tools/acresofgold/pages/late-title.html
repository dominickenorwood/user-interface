Step 5

Late Season (R2 — R6)

Though pollination is in the rear-view mirror, there is still work to be done. As your corn fields transition from lush green to crisp gold, there is a lot to be learned about the successes and failures of management decisions in this year's crop. Intel gathered through walking the fields, from grain fill to maturity, can provide valuable insights that when combined with harvest results may help you dial in on opportunities for improvement in next year's crop.
