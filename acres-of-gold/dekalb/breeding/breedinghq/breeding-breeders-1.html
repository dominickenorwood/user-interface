Delivering DEKALB® seed this year that can outperform the products of last year is the life's work of our breeders – hundreds of men and women around the world with more than 1,700 years of combined experience developing new hybrids and varieties.

Our breeders work in the lab and the field to make hundreds of thousands of crosses between parent plants, and then identify the resulting plants with the best combinations of genes to improve performance.

The development of new products tailored for your farm is our mission. By tapping our global knowledge base and breeders' expertise, we can significantly speed up advancements across our product line–up in the areas that matter to you – like yield, standability, drought tolerance and disease resistance.
