New DEKALB® products are built each year by tapping an expansive foundation of genetic stock, called germplasm. Our germplasm pool pulls genetics from more than 30 countries to consistently supply our breeders with the best genetic "ingredients" and diversity when developing products.

But having an immense amount of genetic material available is only half the solution. On any given day, our breeders, working in hundreds of locations around the globe, are measuring dozens of characteristics looking for a potential new benefit that can ultimately land on your field. Every year, our global breeder network exchanges millions of 'packages' of seed to bring together the best traits from around the globe into new products. So, the DEKALB corn you plant in Illinois may contain genes from plants originally grown far across the world.
