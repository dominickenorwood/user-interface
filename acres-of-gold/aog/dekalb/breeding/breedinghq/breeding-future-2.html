Breeding Science Advancements In The 21st Century

Expand

Continued innovations in research and development are changing the face of plant breeding once again.

The volume of information we can extract from the genetic sequence of corn and the precision with which we can measure visual phenotypes in the field are game-changers in the world of breeding. Years of guessing and planting based only on what we could see in the field have now been replaced by pinpoint accuracy with a computer terminal.

Plant breeding in the 21st century is increasingly reliant on advanced computer and measurement systems to support this data explosion. We have designed our breeding program with the flexibility and scalability to drive these advancements ahead full steam. This biological data mining enables our breeders to identify and improve characteristics that would have been impossible to isolate without these new systems, and this foundation will continue to drive our programs as we move ahead into the next century.
