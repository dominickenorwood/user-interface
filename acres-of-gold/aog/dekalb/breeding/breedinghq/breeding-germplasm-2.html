Year-Round Genetic Research

Speed to MarketThe Benefits of Multi-Season Breeding

With the largest national and international breeding program, DEKALB is able to accelerate the rate of germplasm improvement by growing multiple generations in a single year. Our facilities across the globe let our breeders work year-round to develop and test the best germplasm without any seasonal breaks for different climates.

With seven multi-season program sites, including locations in Hawaii (2), Puerto Rico (2), Mexico, Chile and Costa Rica, seed shipments are coordinated and tracked around the world. In Hawaii, for example, there are three to four corn growing seasons within a calendar year. Our Kunia, Hawaii Research Station has the ability to produce new products year-round, meaning products can be bred more often and get to farmers faster

In addition to multiple growing seasons, this international parentage enables our breeders to pull the best characteristics across the globe, and bring them to your DEKALB® bag. Imagine planting a drought resistant product on your field in Kansas that was developed and field-tested in multiple countries around the world.
