Residue Management

Creating an Ideal Seedbed

Row cleaners are mounted on the planter ahead of the planting unit and are designed to move residue and clods to the side thus clearing path for disk openers to create a true "V" seed trench.

Not Ideal

Uneven emergence in a corn-on-corn field.

Ideal

Properly adjusted row cleaners remove residue from the row and can lead to more consistent planting depth and spacing.

By moving residue and exposing bare soil, row cleaners can help increase soil temperatures in the seed zone and reduce hair-pinning of residue in the seed furrow. Yield increases, fewer barren plants and reduced grain moisture at harvest have all been identified as potential benefits of this type of strip preparation.
