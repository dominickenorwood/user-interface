Product Selection

Evaluation of Product Selection

Scouting during grain fill is important to begin understanding how the individual products and overall package of genetics you've planted are performing in the field.

This is the Timeframe To:

  1. Evaluate pollination success.
  2. Evaluate plant, stalk and ear health.
  3. Estimate yield potential.
  4. Begin developing a harvest schedule to prioritize early maturing, stressed or high-risk fields for lodging to harvest first.

The information gained during this time frame will also help in making product selection decisions for the coming year.
