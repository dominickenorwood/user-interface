Fertility

Soil Sampling & Analysis

Soil tests are recommended at least every 4 years; however, sampling more often can be beneficial in identifying and addressing potential fertility issues impacting yield. In most geographies, fall is the best time to collect and analyze soil samples.

Key Items to consider when collecting soil samples

  • Depth of sample
  • Tillage practices
    • Nutrients may become stratified in no-till and reduced-till systems.
    • If this is the case, a separate soil analysis should be run on the soil from the upper 2" of the soil cores.
  • Time of year
    • For consistency of results samples should be pulled during the same timeframe each year (ideal window is between harvest and when the ground freezes).
